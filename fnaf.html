Five Nights at Freddy's 3

Five Nights at Freddy's 3 is a horror video game and the third installment of the Five Nights at Freddy's series. The game takes place thirty years after the events of the first game in a horror attraction based on the original Freddy Fazbear's Pizza.

In this game, you must survive five nights at Fazbear's Fright: The Horror Attraction. The attraction is centered around the "unfortunate events" that occurred at the original pizza place, and features relics from the old location that have been used to recreate the "suspected" murder scenes.

Your only defense is the security cameras and the maintenance panel, which allows you to seal the vents and reboot systems if they go offline. But be careful - power is limited, and you'll need to manage it carefully to survive until 6 AM.
